Hello, Robot! Introduction to AI and Programming is a course about autonomous navigation algorithms for mobile robots. Students are introduced to the foundations of AI and programming by implementing algorithms for autonomous omni-drive robots.

This course is offered as Robotics 102 at the University of Michigan and is being offered as part of the emerging Michigan Robotics Undergraduate Program. The course is also offered at other institutions as part of a Distributed Teaching Collaborative.
